About This Documentary

The Scientific Revolution represents one of the most transformative periods in human history. Between 1543 and 1687, brilliant minds challenged ancient authorities and established the foundations of modern science through observation, experimentation, and mathematical reasoning.

This documentary explores the thesis, revolution, reform, reaction, impacts, and key discoveries that defined this era. We examine how pioneers challenged established dogma and their commitment to empirical truth continues to inspire scientists and thinkers today.

Navigate through our seven key sections to understand how the Scientific Revolution fundamentally altered humanity's relationship with knowledge and the natural world.
