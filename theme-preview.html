How to Apply a Theme

2. Replace your css/style.css with the corresponding theme file:
css/theme-1-renaissance.css, css/theme-2-academic.css, etc.

Recommendation: Theme 2 (Modern Academic) or Theme 4 (Scientific Enlightenment) offer the best balance of professionalism and readability for educational content about the Scientific Revolution.
